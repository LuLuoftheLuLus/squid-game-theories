Player 246 Theory




Theory Player 246 is still alive and will become a guard in the squid games
Context One

Player 246, Min Jae-seok, is a struggling painter working at a local fair to support his young daughter, who is battling cancer. As a single father overwhelmed by the cost of her life-saving treatment, he joins the Squid Game as a desperate last resort. His love for his daughter drives his every decision, and his creativity and attention to detail, honed through his art, become unexpected strengths in his fight for survival

Gyu-young (Guard 011) works as a mascot at the same fair as Player 246, living out of her car and struggling to make ends meet. She forms a quiet bond with a little girl in a strawberry hat, giving her a lollipop every day they see each other. One day, after quitting her job and walking out of the fair, Gyu-young notices an ambulance rushing back toward where she had just passed. She sees the little girl unconscious, with her distressed father following as they load her into the ambulance. After it speeds away, Gyu-young finds the girl’s strawberry hat on the ground and decides to bring it to the hospital. Later, she is approached by a mysterious man who hands her a card inviting her to the Squid Games—not as a player but as Guard 011, responsible for executing players announced as eliminated
